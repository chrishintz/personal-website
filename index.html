About Chris

I grew up in Seattle, WA. I lived in Atlanta, GA, Denver, CO, and San Jose, CA for eight years before moving back to Seattle in 2014. I was an accountant and consultant at PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) before joining Code Builders to become a developer.

I believe in the continuous development of self and ideas. In my free time, I explore the pacific northwest with my girlfriend, Calla, and my dog, Pawpea.
